Space Exploration Technologies Corp.

Trading as SpaceX, is a private American aerospace manufacturer and space transportation services company headquartered in Hawthorne, California. It was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk with the goal of reducing space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars.

"Our goal is to decrease the cost and improve the reliability of access to space, ultimately by a factor of ten."

- Elon Musk, CEO.

Since June 2010, rockets from the Falcon 9 family have been launched 87 times, with 85 full mission successes.
